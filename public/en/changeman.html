About ChangeMan

Handling change is a prerequisite for all organisations today. Big or small. Our clients are experiencing change in a pace and volume many of them have never seen before. Change in an organization is complex with a multitude of parameters influencing and depending on each other and there is need for managers and change leaders to develop accessible ways to deal with the changes – and to create overview of change at the ‘right’ level of complexity. ChangeMan is a concept and a set of technologies designed to support managers in doing just that. The tools will help the managers map out their changes an make them understand their own role better. At the core of ChangeMan lies HCD’s simulation technologies. These technologies are designed to reach a high level of learning impact within a short time frame. With ChangeMan you can learn fast and change fast. The speed of change is the ultimative competition factor in our business society anno 2014.
