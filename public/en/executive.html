Executive Development Program

As China leaps to global super-power status, HCD Learning believes that demand will become intense for a new generation of creative, skilled leaders who can take the country from a labor-driven economy to tomorrow’s center of global innovation.  HCD Learning’s Executive Program is dedicated to training and unlocking the full potential of tomorrow’s business leaders. 

The Executive Programs design and facilitate programs and Educational Excursions for mid and senior level management that include coursework embedded into its business simulation game experiences.

Advanced Management Program (AMP)

All the professors >

Tailored for Chinese executives, HCD AMP recruits top-notch professors from 1st tier B-schools globally, World Economic Forum guest speakers, and local experts with rich experiences; our AMP can be seen as EMBA in essence.

Target Participants

  • Core Management team member

Advantages compared to EMBA

The courses are selected according to student needs and profiles, straight to the core. Fast-paced program eliminates unnecessary social networking in EMBA, maximizing the knowledge learning within timed- frame, so that student can engage in their work.
Top-notch professors from 1st tier B-school，a convergence of prestigious schools.  Equip you with global vision, as well as insight into China market. Students learn about management framework and theories, and build systematic knowledge.
We assign teaching assistants who manage students’ learning process and progress, including class prep, home work check, and attendance. Follow individual progress closely and provide regular and timely feedback of student performance to HR. 

In-house Design Program

HCD Learning In-house Design program provides highly customized courses to help our clients build up their organizational capabilities for sustainable development. There are two series of courses in this program, one  is the Global Cutting Edge Program and the other one is the Golden Brand Program.

Global Cutting Edge Course

Our  Global Cutting Edge Course  aims to helping international and domestic giant leading companies expand worldwide. We have been pioneering in introducing the most cutting-edge program into China such as Big Data from MIT, Innovation from Stanford D-School etc.These programs put our clients in a position that they can have the same most updated concepts, views and methodologies which have big impact in the business world.  

Golden Brand Program

We also have Golden Brand Courses which target at middle level managers.  Domestic professors & industry experts share their tactical experience and best practices in China to enhance business performance.   Those courses bring direct and immediate benefits to our clients. 

International Learning Excursion

The best way to truly understand local ways and sensitivities is through personal growth gained from direct exposure. HCD Learning leads customized International Learning Excursions for board members,business executives, entrepreneurs and academic delegations with an interest in expanding their knowledge about markets abroad. 

The excursions provide a curated experiential learning opportunity to visit sector-specific companies, industry centers, trade associations, governmental bodies, schools, hospitals, performing arts centers, and more. Each excursion is custom-designed to facilitate introductions with leaders inthe client’s market of interest, as well as taste cultural experiences to better understand local history and society.
