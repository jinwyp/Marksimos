LeaderStyle

LeaderStyle is designed to broaden the perspectives of managers living in today’s globalized world. The modern manager has to be able to lead the business, lead others and lead himself/herself. This requires focus and most of all: self-awareness. With LeaderStyle the manager can become aware of the repertoire or ‘span’ which he/she can cover. We achieve this self-awareness through our learning design where we broaden the leader’s mind instead of testing it or putting the leader in a ‘box’.

We teach the managers to step into the areas where they feel uncomfortable and to take the full advantage of their strengths. We teach them how to understand the small situations, but also the bigger sequences of leadership involved in large scale changes. LeaderStyle is a combination of physical games, computer based simulations and interactive challenges, context-scenarios, theoretical models and visualizations. A great time for managers and a huge learning leap!

The styles describe the manager’s modus operandi:

  • Commanding Leadership Style:
    1

    Demands immediate compliance

  • Pace-setting Leadership Style:
    2

    Sets high standards for performance

  • Visionary Leadership Style :
    3

    Mobilizes people towards a vision

  • Democratic Leadership Style :
    4

    Forges consensus through participation

  • Affiliative Leadership Style :
    5

    Create harmony and emotional bonds

  • Coaching Leadership Style :
    6

    Develops people for the future
